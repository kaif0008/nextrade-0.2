Privacy Policy

At NexTrade, we are committed to protecting your business and personal data through transparency and security.

Introduction

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how NexTrade ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our B2B marketplace platform. By using the platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

  • •Account Information: Name, business name, email, phone number, and password.
  • •Business Data: GST number, industry, business photos, and establishment details.
  • •Trading Data: Negotiated deals, product requirements, and chat history.
  • •Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and platform usage statistics via Nexa.

How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • •Provide and maintain the NexTrade marketplace services.
  • •Facilitate negotiations between wholesalers and shopkeepers.
  • •Improve our AI assistant, Nexa, to better serve your business needs.
  • •Verify the legitimacy of businesses on our platform to prevent fraud.

Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your sensitive business information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@nextrade.com.
