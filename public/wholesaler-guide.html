Wholesaler Guide

Master the NexTrade platform to scale your distribution and manage bulk orders with ease.

Getting Started

1

Create Your Account

Register as a Wholesaler. You will need a valid GST number and business details. Upload a professional business banner to attract more retailers.

2

Verify Your Business

Verification is key to building trust. Complete your profile, verify your email, and ensure your GST details are accurate. Verified wholesalers get 5x more inquiries.

3

Inventory Management

Use your Wholesaler Dashboard to add products. Set clear pricing, Minimum Order Quantities (MOQ), and current stock levels. High-quality images (under 5MB) are highly recommended.

Handling Inquiries & Deals

The core of NexTrade is our unique negotiation system. When a retailer is interested in your product, they will send an inquiry or a "Deal Proposal".

  • •Real-time Messaging: Use the messaging portal to discuss logistics and customization.
  • •Price Updates: You can update the offered price based on the bulk quantity requested.
  • •Confirming Deals: Once both parties agree, mark the deal as "Accepted". This creates a persistent record for your business analytics.

💡 Pro Tips for Success

Use Nexa: Don't forget to consult your AI assistant, Nexa. She can provide demand insights, telling you which products are trending and how many retailers are currently looking for items in your category.
